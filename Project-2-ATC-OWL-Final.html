Owl

Owls are captivating creatures revered for their enigmatic nature and remarkable adaptations.

  • Scientific Name: Strigiformes
  • Average Length: 20 inches
  • Average Lifespan: 20 years
  • Habitat: Diverse (Forest, Grasslands, Urban areas)

From the haunting call of the great horned owl echoing through the night to the majestic flight of the barn owl gliding silently over fields, owls embody the mystique and wonder of the natural world, captivating hearts and minds with their timeless allure.
